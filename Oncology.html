Oncology Department

HOME > ABOUT US > PATIENT > DEPARTMENTS > CARDIOLOGY > DERMATOLOGY > NEUROLOGY > UROLOGY > ONCOLOGY

Cancer treatment with international standards and clinical excellence at your hands reach with Department of Oncology. With multidisciplinary approach, latest technologies and decades of experience

the facilities offered are mentioned below:

  • Medical Oncology
  • Surgical Oncology
  • Oncopathology – FNAC & Biopsy
  • Immunohistochemistry
  • Chemotherapy with laminar air flow
  • Interventional radiology – Radio Frequency Ablation, TACE
  • Day care unit
  • Multi-disciplinary tumour board
  • Blood bank with facilities for component therapy
  • Cancer nutrition and lifestyle counselling

With the endeavour to detect cancer early so that lives could be saved, we offer a spectrum of cancer detection tests:

  • Cervical cancer – PAP smear
  • Colorectal cancer – Colonoscopy
  • Stomach cancer – Gastroscopy
  • Breast cancer – USG with elastography and Digital mammography
  • Prostate cancer – PSA
  • Lung cancer – HRCT
  • Ovarian cancer – USG & CA 125
  • Liver cancer – USG & AFP

The centre also engages itself in different academic activities, awareness programmes, case discussions, seminars and multi-disciplinary medical boards like the tumour board.
