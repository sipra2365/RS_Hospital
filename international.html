FACILITIES FOR INTERNATIONAL PATIENTS

Services & Facilities

Accommodation :
There is a wide range of hotels, guest houses available in the vicinity.
Airport Pick up and Drop :
We offer Airport pick up and drop for International Patients who need transportation through ambulance on request.
Dedicated Relationship Managers :
To offer the feel of home away from home, we have Relationship Managers at different points of our service delivery process.
