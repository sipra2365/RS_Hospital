Plastic Surgery Department

HOME > ABOUT US > PATIENT > DEPARTMENTS > CARDIOLOGY > DERMATOLOGY > NEUROLOGY > UROLOGY > ONCOLOGY > PLASTIC SURGERY

The Department provides comprehensive treatment for all types of plastic and cosmetic procedures.

It has evolved as one of the finest disciplines routinely performing complex surgeries. The strength of the department apart from well trained doctors lies in the teamwork along with protocol based patient care. Our efforts have made us one of the most sought after centres for plastic surgery treatments in Eastern India.

Our Services

Cosmetic Facial Surgery

  • Face lift (Rhytidectomy)
  • Mid-face lift
  • Neck lift
  • Forehead/Eyebrow lift
  • Eyelid surgery (Blepharoplasty)
  • Cosmetic nose surgery (Rhinoplasty)
  • Cosmetic ear surgery (Otoplasty)
  • Facial implants & chin surgery
Facial Resurfacing & Rejuvenation
  • Chemical peels
  • Dermabrasion & Microdermabrasion
Breast Surgery
  • Breast augmentation
  • Breast lift (Mastopexy)
  • Breast reduction
Body Contouring Surgery
  • Tummy tuck (Abdominoplasty) & Panniculectomy
  • Surgery after significant weight loss
  • Body lift
  • Arm lift
  • Thigh lift
  • Buttock lift
  • Genital Surgery including Hypospadias
  • Transgender surgery
  • Surgery for Gynaecomastia
We have also provided remedial solutions to numerous patients successfully in the following areas:
  • Burns
  • Birth defects
  • Hand surgery
  • White patches
  • Kelloids
  • Scar revision
