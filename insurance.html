INSURANCE FACILITIES

"CASHless treatment facility available through Insurance Company."

"Presentation of the valid current year insurance policy / TPA card at the admission desk / PR desk are essential during admission."

"In case of emergency admission non availability of policy/TPA card, same has to be produce and photo copy given within 24hrs. of admission as per IRDA norms."

"Approval of cash less treatment from appropriate authority is mandatory."

"In occassion of Denial of cashless treatment facility from the TPA, the bill has to be paid in full either through Cash/ Credit Card / Debit Card / Demand Draft at the time of final settlement of bills before discharge."

"For further assistance, please contact our Public Relations Department at."

+91 33 4011 1222 (Extension No. 5718 / 5719)
