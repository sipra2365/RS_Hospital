NURSING

For the purpose of ensuring proper and systematic training, Peerless Hospital and B.K Roy Research Centre commissioned the Peerless Institute Of Nursing in the year 2006.
The Institute Of Nursing is recognized by Indian Nursing Council, New Delhi and West Bengal Nursing Council and is affiliated to the West Bengal University of Health Sciences.
As per Indian Nursing Council norms, the objectives of The College of Nursing are to prepare the graduate nurses who will- -assume responsibilities as competent nurses within the framework of code of ethics and professional conduct, - provide nursing care to individuals, families and the community.
The passed out graduates are also expected to assume the role of teacher, supervisor in the clinical or public health arena.

POST BASIC NSC NURSING COURSE
ELIGIBILITY:FemaleAnnual Intake: 40 candidates with 10+2 and General Nursing & Midwifery qualification along with the service experience as per rules of the West Bengal University of Health Sciences.
Duration: 2 Years
Annual Intake: 40
